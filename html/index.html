FAQ

What documents are required to rent a car?

+

To rent a car, you will need a valid driver's license, a government-issued ID (such as a passport or national ID), and a credit card for the security deposit. Some locations may also require proof of insurance. Please ensure all documents are up-to-date and present them during the booking process.

What are the car cancellation and refund policies?

+

Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the rental period will receive a full refund, while those made less than 24 hours in advance may be eligible for a partial refund, depending on the circumstances. No refunds are provided for cancellations made after the rental period has started

Do you offer car delivery and pickup services?

+

Yes, we offer convenient car delivery and pickup services. You can choose to have the car delivered to your preferred location and picked up when you're done. Additional charges may apply based on the distance and location. Please let us know your requirements during the booking process, and we will be happy to assist!

What should i do in case of an accident or car damage?

+

In case of an accident or car damage, ensure your safety first and contact emergency services if needed. Then, notify our support team immediately for assistance. Gather all necessary details, including photos of the damage, contact information of involved parties, and a police report if required. Follow the instructions provided by our team to handle insurance claims and further steps.

What is the minimum age to rent a luxury car?

+

The minimum age to rent a luxury car is typically 25 years. However, this may vary depending on the car model and rental location. Drivers under 25 might be subject to additional fees or restrictions. Please check the specific requirements during the booking process.

What is included in the rental price?

+

The rental price typically includes the base rental fee, standard mileage allowance, and basic insurance coverage. Additional services, such as GPS, child seats, or premium insurance, may be available at extra cost. Fuel and toll charges are generally not included. Please review your booking details for a complete breakdown of what is included.

Are there any additional costs i should be aware of?

+

Yes, there may be additional costs depending on your rental. These can include charges for extra mileage, fuel refills, late returns, additional drivers, premium insurance, or optional accessories like GPS and child seats. Delivery and pickup services, as well as fees for young or senior drivers, may also apply. Please review your rental agreement for a detailed breakdown.

Do the cars come with insurance? What does it cover?

+

Yes, all our rental cars come with basic insurance, which typically covers third-party liability and limited coverage for damages to the rental car. However, this may not include full coverage for theft, personal injuries, or damages caused by negligence. For more comprehensive protection, additional insurance options are available at the time of booking. Please review the policy details for specific coverage information.
